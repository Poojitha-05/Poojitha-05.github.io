Calculation Assumptions - Operational Boundaries
  1. The prices listed in the .xlsx spreadheet in the link above were used to standardize pricing for fuels. The CO2e emissions (including methane) are assumed to be 1% of the CO2 emissions as listed in the sheet. For waste, the emissions per person is assumed to be 692 lbs and their waste reduction is deducted from this total to acheive the total emissions per person. The formulas used do not account for leap years. To learn more about the exact emissions data, please see the link corresponding to this footnote.
  2. The eGRID database contains recent estimates of the electricity emission factors of each state, which was used to determine electricity emissions.
  3. The data per capita was used to calculate the average emissions for each state.
  4. The meat products listed on this sheet were accounted for in the "Waste" solutions > "Tree" section > "Vegetarian diet"
